Sisterly

Sisterly is a term coined by my sisters Achi, Jasmine and Blossom. It is used to refer to one's sister while still showing a deeper level of connection, bonding and relatable understanding between sisters. They coined it at a time when we all needed the co-operation of each sister to perform a certain task.

It's not like the regular terms for referring to one's siter like 'sissy' or 'sister' or even 'sis.' It shows how stron the bond between sisters is, what that bond could become and how each sister can relate to the next one in very many unique ways.
